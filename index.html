Our Story

Every love story is beautiful, but ours is our favorite

First Meeting

September 2019

We met at a coffee shop in downtown San Francisco. Michael was reading a book, and Sarah couldn't help but notice it was her favorite novel.

First Date

October 2019

Our first official date was at a cozy Italian restaurant. We talked for hours and knew there was something special between us.

March 2021

We decided to take the next step and move in together. Our little apartment became our sanctuary and the place where our love grew stronger.

The Proposal

December 2023

Michael proposed during a romantic weekend getaway in Napa Valley. Sarah said yes without hesitation, and we've been planning our dream wedding ever since.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our wedding day

What is the dress code?

We're asking for cocktail attire - think semi-formal and elegant. Ladies can wear a nice dress or dressy separates, and gentlemen can wear a suit or dress shirt with slacks.

Will the ceremony be outdoors?

Yes! The ceremony will be held in the beautiful vineyard amphitheater. We have a covered backup location in case of inclement weather.

Is there parking available?

Yes, there is complimentary parking available at the venue. We also have shuttle service from the recommended hotels.

Can I bring a guest?

We love your friends and family, but we're planning an intimate celebration. Please check your invitation for the number of guests you can bring.

Will there be vegetarian/vegan options?

Absolutely! We'll have vegetarian and vegan options available. Please let us know about any dietary restrictions when you RSVP.

What time should I arrive?

We recommend arriving at 3:45 PM for the ceremony. This gives you time to find your seat and enjoy the pre-ceremony atmosphere.
